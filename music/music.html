How I got into music and since when I make it

I've always been a person that listened to a lot of music. In the last few years that has only increased. Because I'm somebody who's quite sensitive to sounds and easily gets stressed, music often helps me calm down and get in a good state of mind. So in december 2022, I decided to start making music and I downloaded FL Studio. As expected, the first few weeks/months were kinda hard and i lost motivation pretty quickly. I often had and still have phases where I won't make music for like a month straight, then go into making like 5 songs in a week. Getting motivation can be kinda hard for me, but whenever I actually get to making music I really enjoy it, as it truly is my biggest passion.
